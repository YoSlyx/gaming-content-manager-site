🎮 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

GamingContentManager ("the App") is a personal content management tool designed to help individual TikTok gaming creators schedule, organize, and automate their content publishing workflow.

2. Data We Access

This application may access your TikTok account information including: your public profile, video upload capabilities, and content metadata (titles, descriptions, hashtags). This data is used exclusively to manage and schedule your TikTok content.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your personal device. We do not transmit your personal data to any third-party servers. Your TikTok credentials and content data remain on your machine at all times.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Data shared with TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. Since data is stored locally, you are responsible for your device's security.

6. User Rights

You may revoke access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings. You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the application.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app developer through GitHub: github.com/YoSlyx
